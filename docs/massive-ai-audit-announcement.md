# Massive AI Audit is live — try it now

Massive AI Audit is ready for you to test. We’d love your feedback before we roll it out more broadly.

## What’s new

- **Run AI audits across many agents at once** — no more running them one by one.
- **Simple flow:** choose agents (with Intercom ID), date range, and scorecard, then click **Run AI Audit**.
- **Live progress:** you see exactly where things stand (e.g. “3 of 10 agents done”, “150 of 2000 chats audited”).
- **Runs on the server:** you can close the tab and come back later — the audit keeps running.

## Pro tip: prompts drive the results

The audit uses the prompts from your scorecard. Clearer, more specific prompts lead to better AI output. It’s worth a few minutes to review and refine your scorecard prompts before you run a big audit.

## Where to try it

**CQMS → Audit Distribution → AI**

Select agents, set dates and scorecard, then click **Run AI Audit**. You’ll land on the progress/results page for that run.
